# Marketing Analytics Dashboard

Data-driven analysis of marketing performance and ROI optimization

---

## Overview

Conducted a comprehensive analysis of marketing campaign performance to identify key drivers of return on investment (ROI) and uncover optimization opportunities.

## Objective

Determine which marketing channels generate the highest returns and identify inefficiencies in budget allocation.

## Data

Simulated marketing dataset including campaign spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, and revenue.

## Methodology

* Cleaned and structured raw campaign data
* Calculated key performance metrics (CTR, Conversion Rate, ROI)
* Analyzed channel-level performance
* Visualized trends and performance differences across campaigns

## Results

* Identified top-performing channels driving the highest ROI
* Detected underperforming campaigns with inefficient spend
* Highlighted opportunities to reallocate budget for improved performance

## Key Takeaways

Strategic reallocation of marketing spend toward high-performing channels can significantly improve overall conversion efficiency and ROI.

## Tools

Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) / Excel
