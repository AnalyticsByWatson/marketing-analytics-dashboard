# Spotify Market Analysis Dashboard

Data-driven analysis of search demand trends in the music streaming industry

---

## Overview

Conducted a data-driven market analysis of the music streaming industry using search demand data to evaluate platform growth, competitive positioning, and event-driven behavior.

---

## Objective

Analyze how user demand evolves over time across major streaming platforms and identify key drivers of growth and engagement.

---

## Data

* Multi-platform search demand data (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music)
* Weekly time-series data spanning multiple years
* Aggregated and structured for trend and comparative analysis

---

## Methodology

* Structured multi-source datasets into a unified time series
* Used pivot tables to track platform-level performance over time
* Analyzed seasonal and event-driven demand spikes
* Created layered visualizations to isolate growth trends and competition

---

## Results

* Spotify demonstrates strong and accelerating growth

  * Search demand increases from low-40s (2021) to peaks near 80 (2022)

* Market growth is uneven across platforms

  * Spotify dominates total demand
  * Apple Music and YouTube Music remain stable but smaller contributors

* Demand is highly event-driven

  * Annual spikes align with Spotify Wrapped
  * These events generate the largest traffic surges in the dataset

* Post-event demand stabilizes at higher levels

  * Indicates sustained long-term growth, not temporary spikes

---

## Key Takeaway

The music streaming market is driven by strategic, high-impact events that capture large-scale consumer attention. Platforms that effectively own these moments generate sustained growth advantages.
